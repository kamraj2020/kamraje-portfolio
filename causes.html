Causes and Solutions 

        

There are numerous causes of air pollution that makes the air
unhealthy and dangerous for the people, not just humans but 
also affects birds and plants.    To start off one of the
most reckless causes is man-made sources that contribute to 
the contamination of air. 
Man-made causes are things that humans do or create which 
now affects the environment. Man-made sources:

*Fossil Fuels Emissions 

*Industrial emissions

*Improper dump of waste   

   
    
How does this affect humans:
Air pollution can cause a lot of lifelong illness and health 
effects on humans. If the  air we are breathing in  is unsafe
and toxic, it will definitely affect our respiratory system. 
A individual can have:

        *Lung cancer
        *Heart disease
        *Asthma 
        *Strokes 
        *Damage of nerves, brain, liver, kidney and so 
        many other organs. 
        *Also, it has been proven that unhealthy air can
        cause birth defects. 
        *Death of humans 
        
  
       How can we help the situation?
           
*Reduce the number of trips you take in your car
*Try using electric cars or a car with greater fuel efficiency 
*Avoid burning trash that can be recycle 
*Turn off appliances when they are not in use
*Avoid smoking 
*Use a fan instead of an air conditioner
Shop locally at farmers market 
*Manage and reduce emissions 
*Buy venerable energy 
*Conserve energy in your daily lives
